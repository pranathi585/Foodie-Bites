📖 My Kitchen Story

Born from late-night cravings and a lot of burned pans, Foodie Bites began as a hobby and became my personal food diary. From experimenting in a tiny dorm kitchen to curating full-course meals — I’ve grown with every spill and stir.

🌐 About This Website

Foodie Bites is a cozy digital kitchen for food lovers of all kinds. It's filled with easy, comforting recipes, kitchen tips, and flavor-packed ideas to turn everyday ingredients into magic.

You’ll find recipes rooted in tradition, spiced with creativity, and always served with a story.

  • 🍽️ Step-by-step recipes with real ingredients
  • 📸 Plating + photography tips
  • 🌱 Budget + beginner friendly options
  • 🌏 A mix of Indian classics & global dishes
